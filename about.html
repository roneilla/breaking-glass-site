We are a powerful team of womxn looking to uplift and inspire others

Mission Statement
Our mission is to deliver workshops and longer-term programs that build womxn-founded companies into strong, market-leading brands, and build on founder's skills in networking, communications and marketing, so that the world will embrace their great ideas.

One of our original inspirations for building Breaking Glass was to fill a gap in what Womxn needed and wanted out of their working lives Womxn can face a range of challenges expressing and asking for workplace needs in 9-5 jobs.

We felt it was key to ensure they were also able to provide this for themselves, and their employees at their new companies.

What motivates us to keep going

Even on a more granular level, we found that when we looked at data around womxn in the workplace, even the way womxn were described differed from their male counterparts. In the start-up context, this can influence grants, funding from investors, and partnerships.

We wanted to change the way womxn are perceived, and give power back to womxn founders.

We're pushing for representation, and embracing womxn as founders and leaders

Womxn have been historically underrepresented in leadership roles, and in founder or owner positions. But it doesn't have to be this way. Across all industries, we can shift this trend. This is what motivates us to keep growing our programs at Breaking Glass.
